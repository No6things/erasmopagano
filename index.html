About Us

Designer and Manufacturer
of Stylish Ladies Shoes

  • Our Company was founded in 2004 by the hand of famous recognized Italian Shoe Stylist Erasmo Pagano
  • In 2014, we established a fully owned Subsidiary in the United Kingdom from where we will service this bouyant and appealing market for our shoe style
  • Erasmo has been responsible for the designs of the finest woman shoes under brand like:
    Lady Ana, Camila Stile, Gentile, Ben Hur, Ferradini, Wuao and many others
  • We are now manufacturing from Portugal and China
    We have open a Distribution facility in the United Kingdom
    We are in capacity to export our own brands anywhere in the World

What we do!!

  • We design and manufacture high standard fashion ladies shoes
    for the modern women.
  • Our products are mostly made in leather but sometimes
    we combine our styles with new fabrics, synthetics or other accesories that make up our styles unique.
  • We have been for over 40 years in Business of shoe modeling and manufacturing staying abreast current fashion trends.
  • The quality of our products is based on a manual process that is permanently under scrutiny of our own Stylist.
  • Our designs take into account the demands and feedback of our Clients.
